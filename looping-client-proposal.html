About the Project
What is Looping?

Looping is a mobile application that allows users to discover and connect with real, verified people who are physically present near them. Whether you are at a college campus, a networking event, a co-working space, or a cafe, Looping shows you who is around you on a live radar screen and lets you reach out to them instantly.

Every user is required to verify their identity using government-issued documents through DigiLocker. Once two users connect, they can chat in real time and make voice and video calls - all within the app, without exchanging personal contact details.

Looping is a trust-first networking tool built around real-world presence, verified identity, and privacy you control completely.

The Problem
People are surrounded by potential connections every day but have no safe, verified way to reach out. Existing apps rely on online networks, not real-world presence.
The Solution
Looping uses GPS and identity verification to show a live view of verified users nearby, giving people a trusted and private way to connect in person.

Looping Core Capabilities
Core Capabilities
Everything the app does, explained clearly.
01
GPS-Based Proximity Discovery
An animated radar screen shows verified users within 500 metres of your location in real time. Nearby users appear as bubbles on the radar, each showing their name and distance. The closer they are, the closer their bubble appears to the centre. Tap any bubble to view a mini profile and send a connection request.
02
User Registration and Login
New users complete a guided 5-step sign-up covering profile details, phone OTP verification, purpose selection, social account linking and privacy preferences. Returning users log in with phone OTP, email and password, or biometric authentication such as fingerprint or Face ID.
03
Connection Requests
Tap a radar bubble to send a connection request. The receiver is notified instantly and can accept or decline. Unanswered requests expire after 48 hours. Once accepted, a private chat and calling are unlocked between both users. Any user can be blocked or reported instantly.
04
Real-Time Private Chat
Messages are delivered instantly. The chat supports text, emoji and image sharing. Every message shows a delivery state: sent, delivered and read, similar to WhatsApp's tick system. A live typing indicator appears when the other person is writing. Screenshots are disabled inside the chat screen.
05
Voice and Video Calls
High-quality 1-to-1 voice and video calls powered by Twilio. Video supports picture-in-picture, allowing you to minimise the call into a small floating window while continuing to use your phone. A full call history with type, duration and missed call details is saved in the app.
06
Identity Verification
Looping uses DigiLocker, the Government of India's official platform, to verify each user's Aadhaar, PAN card, college ID or office ID. Verified documents appear as badges on the profile. Raw Aadhaar numbers are never stored - only the last four digits are kept, in compliance with the Aadhaar Act 2016.
07
Privacy and Safety Controls
Users control what others see: name, phone, email, occupation, education and social accounts. Discovery mode can be switched off at any time, making the user completely invisible. Any user can be blocked or reported. Fully compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023.
08
Push Notifications and Alerts
Instant notifications for connection requests, accepted requests, new messages and incoming calls. Incoming calls trigger a native phone-call-style screen on both iPhone and Android even when the app is closed. All notification preferences are individually manageable from the settings screen.

Application server
Powers the entire backend of Looping. Handles all requests from the app, manages user data, processes real-time messaging and executes business logic. Used by LinkedIn, PayPal and Uber.
